gweifields is an experimental, dynamic NFT project with no roadmap or promises, just art made with care. Gweifields is less concerned with a permanent controlled beauty or surface level aesthetics. But rather highlights through ongoing, momentary representations, a relationship of flux between NFT object and Ethereum Mainnet.

Network activity is measured by gweifields as two variables;

1. ETH Price
2. Gas Price (Gwei)

Updated every minute, both variables are used to generate color within the work - Gas price being used to generate the background color, while ETH price generates the central object color.
